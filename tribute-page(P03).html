Biography of Johannes Vermeer

Johannes Vermeer (1632–1675) was a Dutch Baroque painter renowned for his masterful handling of light and his intimate scenes of domestic life. Born and based in Delft, he left only about 35 known works, including Girl with a Pearl Earring, The Milkmaid, and View of Delft. Girl with a Pearl Earring

Little recognized in his lifetime and beset by financial difficulty, Vermeer’s quiet brilliance was largely forgotten until the 19th century. Today he is celebrated for exquisite technique, optical subtlety, and poetic stillness.
